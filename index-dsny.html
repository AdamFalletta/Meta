How It Works: Clean Streets in 3 Verified Steps

1.

Issue a Task Ticket

A cleanup need (debris, litter, illegal dumping) is reported via 311 or the DSNY integrated system. This task is immediately geo-tagged and prioritized.

2.

Local Taskforce Responds

The nearest certified Squad member is alerted via the mobile app. They accept the task and are instantly dispatched to the hyper-local site.

3.

Verified & Compensated

Completion is verified by DSNY via timestamped photos and GPS data uploaded through the app. The worker receives instant, pre-set payment.

How We Fund a Cleaner Future

1. City Task Funding: Micro-Contracting with DSNY

This is the core, high-reliability revenue stream. DSNY allocates a dedicated budget for verified on-demand cleanup tasks that fall outside standard routing schedules.

  • Each cleanup incident (debris, minor dumping) is billed at a fixed, pre-approved micro-rate ($25‚Äì$75).
  • Tasks are documented with before/after evidence for transparent billing.
  • **Justification:** Significantly more cost-effective than diverting full DSNY crews for minor incidents.

4. Foundation & Green Infrastructure Grants

Our model aligns perfectly with foundations focused on urban job creation, environmental resiliency, and community beautification. This stream targets large, multi-year funding to secure program longevity.

We prioritize grants related to urban resilience, waste management innovation, economic mobility through employment, and neighborhood health and safety initiatives.

By rapidly clearing debris, we ensure proper function of storm drains and prevent runoff pollution, directly supporting city green infrastructure goals.

8. Urban Data Licensing & Insights

Every task is geo-tagged and timestamped, creating a unique dataset of urban sanitation needs. We ethically license this aggregated, non-personal data to third parties.

  • **Clients:** City planning, environmental research, infrastructure analysts, and insurance firms.
  • **Purpose:** Identifying blight hotspots, optimizing city resource deployment, and proactive planning.
