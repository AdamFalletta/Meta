A fully integrated ecosystem bridging advanced semiconductor technology, AI orchestration, sports innovation, interactive entertainment, cloud infrastructure, and immersive fan experiences.

2026 Executive Summary

1. Corporate Vision & Core Philosophy

  • Mission: Build an interconnected ecosystem spanning advanced computing, interactive entertainment, sports innovation, AI, and cloud infrastructure.
  • Philosophy: Leverage creativity, technical mastery, and operational orchestration to produce fully integrated systems that merge physical, digital, and experiential realms.
  • Strategic Focus: High-scale modular expansion, recurring revenue via software, cloud services, IP licensing, and gamified experiences.

2. Flagship Hardware & Semiconductor Innovation

  • RevV-X Chiplets & NEO Processing Units (NPUs): Fabless semiconductor powering NeoGear, Knockout!, MetaOps devices, OCX1 modules; embedded sensors and processing for ultra-low-latency computation.
  • OCX1 & ACA1 Modules: Core MCU and SPI-linked devices integrating NeoGear, MetaOps, and arcade platforms; enables real-time orchestration and cloud-device communication.

3. MetaOS & CloudPress AI Infrastructure

  • MetaOS: Centralized OS integrating hardware, cloud services, AI orchestration, and application ecosystems.
  • CloudPress AI Studio & Plugins: Enterprise/gaming analytics, cloud resilience, predictive AI monitoring, multi-cloud orchestration, custom plugin deployment.
  • Purpose: Spine for all AFI Systems, blending hardware, LLM-powered analytics, and orchestration across physical and virtual platforms.

4. NeoBaseball & Sports Innovation

  • NeoBaseball League & Purple Eagles Team: Sports IP integrating gameplay, tech-embedded gear, and fan gamification.
  • NeoGear System: NeoBat, NeoBall, NEOGlove, RevV-X tech tracking performance, game data, fan modifiers.
  • NEOCenter & NEOSports Network: Stadiums, streaming, analytics, immersive hubs.
  • Gamification Ecosystem: NEOCards, online modifiers, AI-driven mechanics.
  • Rule Innovations & Governance: Adam as commissioner driving rules and operations.

5. Knockout! & Interactive Entertainment

  • Knockout!: Physical-to-digital hybrid arcade experience with large displays and IR sensors.
  • Perfect Game System: Real-time scoring and analytics integration.
  • Modular Cabinet Builds: DIY to professional-grade systems for global rollout.
  • Integration: RevV-X processors and CloudPress for leaderboard and streaming.

6. BlabBot & MetaCommunications

  • BlabBot AI Platform: Enterprise/consumer conversational AI integrated into MetaOS and CloudPress.
  • MetaCommunications Suite: Unified messaging, AI interaction, and user-context analytics across AFI platforms.
  • Purpose: Enhances fan engagement, workflow, and cross-platform orchestration linking NeoSports, Space Rage, and MetaOS.

7. Space Rage & Global Arcade Expansion

  • Space Rage Arcade Cabinets: Free-to-play with online head-to-head competition, recurring ad revenue (~$360/unit/year).
  • Global Rollout: 100,000 units across 25 countries, $20,000 per unit.
  • Integration: CloudPress analytics, predictive AI for maintenance/network orchestration.
  • Expansion: Lessons from Knockout! enable scalable immersive experiences globally.

8. R&D & Future Hardware Development

  • NEOBox & ACA1 Modules: Experimental multi-sensor, multi-device orchestration.
  • Next-Gen Sensors & Game Interfaces embedded across sports, arcades, and MetaOS devices.
  • Innovation Focus: Physical-to-digital bridge, AI decision-making, real-time interactive control.
  • Prototyping & Testing: Iterative builds across Knockout!, NeoBaseball, and Space Rage.

9. Integrated AI & Orchestration

  • NeoBaseballOps: Orchestrates rules, AI modifiers, performance analytics.
  • MetaOS AI Orchestration: Integrates CloudPress plugins, NPUs, hardware modules.
  • Data Flow: Sensor inputs → NPUs → MetaOS → CloudPress → Fan/Enterprise dashboards.
  • Outcome: Fully automated system linking player action, fan interaction, enterprise analytics, global cloud orchestration.
